infrasturcute & facilities

Food Court

The Sanjay Ghodawat Group of Institution's Campus has a Centrally Located Food Court that caters to the Culinary needs of the students and staff.The huge circular tent like structure is one the many centers of attraction in the 150 acres campus full of infrastructure wonders.It can accommodate around 2000 students at a time .Currently there are 3 cateres who provide food mess facility to the students and also different outlets that provide fast food varieties like Idli, Dosa, Chinese, Burger, Pizzas, Ice-Creams, Nescafe etc. This is a place where you find the students and staff love to hang out after their day of hard work and they do find it a place where they get refreshed, Refilled and recharged.

morning activities

Walking Track

Walking is the best form exercise one can count on. Students are here made aware of the same and regularly encouraged to walk on the walking track early in the morning. A Period of 45 minutes is scheduled everyday for the same and students enthusiastically participate with the teaching staff

Morning Yoga

Holistic Development of students is one of the aims of the S G I M A . Yoga for physical and mental faculty development is practiced for students so that they gain strength stamina,flexibility and concentration to excel in their career. Yoga class everyday is conducted for 45 min for the students under the guidance of a Yoga Teacher

Sports

Every Saturday & Sunday is marked for sports activities in the S G I M A campus. Staurday is scheduled for the Girl students and Sunday for the Boys. Students involve in sports activities like Cricket, Football,Basket ball,Tennis , Volley Ball etc.After rigorous academic schedule for the week,sport activities provide the necessary recreational relief and charge them up.

infrastructure

Amphitheater

With the host of facilities and amenities available at SGI the amphitheater is not a surprise addition. Students find a place to explore their creative side at the SGI amphitheater. It is a right place to host various extra curricular and co curricular activities like dramatics,speeches ,debetes etc.It is place where student's overalldevelopment of personality is honed and honored. It gives the students a stage to discover their potential

Auditorium

SGI has a state of the art and fully equipped modern auditorium with a seating capacity of over 200. The auditorium is equipped with air conditioning sysytem quality system,acoustics,CCTV,LCD projectors,comfortable seating system.All the major functions for the tudents and the staff are held here at the auditorium. Aesthetics of the auditorium hve been carefully cosidered giving it the right nuances of comfort,beauty and quality

Conference Room

'If you fail to plan, you plan to fail.'Any organization requires meticulous planning back up for its sysytems to be streamlined aptly,it is here that the importance of committee meets come into picture. SGI has a state of the art conference hall that is fully equipped with ultra modern amenities to facilitate such important meets.The conference is replate with hi-definiation audio system,LCD projector air conditioning and many more other facilities.Important meets are held periodically at the Conference Room. It is one of the important features of the institute from where strategies and decisions are implemented.

Hostel

SGI is situated in a scenic and serene location away from the din and bustle of city life.A location like this will bring up the concern of accommodation as commuting everyday may be time consuming for many students. It is to counter this concern,Hostel facilities for Boys and Girls are made available in the campus of SGI. SGI hosts separate Boys and Girls Hostels.The Hostel is in many sense a home away from home for the students ,replete with many facilities like like Wi-Fi and net connectivity,sports facilities,computer laboratories, common study room,attached bath with hot water facility,lift with power back up system.One of the issues that comes up is of frequent power cuts which are countered by a 24 hours back up supply so that student's activities and studies are not interrupted . In addition to all these facilities , the Hostel also has 24 hours round the clock security, dedicated wardens, medical doctor round he clock with an ambulance facility for emergencies and also an anti-ragging committee to ensure the security of students

Parking

Sanjay Ghodawat Institutes has ample space reserved for parking of vehicles-2 wheeler and 4-wheelers . The parking of space is separately marked for 2 & 4 wheelers Students and the Staff can be rest assured that their vehicles are safe and secure in the space provided for the parking. The parking area is also under security surveillance to prevent theft damage and other untowards incidents.

In House Clinic

Going by the adage,"Health is Wealth," SGIIT MA provides for an In-Houe Clinic with trained and qualified medical officer and staff to cater for health emergencies of the students and staff. The In-House Clinic is well equipped to look after primary health emergencies that one could encounter. The In-House Clinic also has the provision of a well equipped ambulance in order to convey medical emergencies to nearest hospital
